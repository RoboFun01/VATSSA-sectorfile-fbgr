===== FBSK Departure =====

  * FBSK doesn't have a dedicated Departure Position, with Gaborone Approach (FBSK APP) assuming the role of Departure.


==== Departure Procedures ====

  * After departure, climb the aircraft via the SID to FL145 or FL245 (coordinate with FBGR_CTR).
  * If aircraft departing via runwayheading, provide further climb to MORA before vectoring.
  * Be aware of High Terrain (MSA of 9500 ft) E of the field.
