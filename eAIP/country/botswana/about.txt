==== Botswana Information ====

Botswana, officially the Republic of Botswana, is a landlocked country in Southern Africa. Botswana is topographically flat, with approximately 70 percent of its territory being the Kalahari Desert. It is bordered by [[country:south-africa:home|South Africa]] to the south and southeast, [[country:namibia:home|Namibia]] to the west and north, and [[country:zimbabwe:home|Zimbabwe]] to the northeast.
